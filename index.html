ABOUT US

Welcome to Your Sisterhood in the City!

We're a community of women in Johannesburg who come together to move, create, and connect. Whether you love running, knitting, painting, pilates, or simply making new friends — this is your circle. Our club is for women of all ages, all backgrounds, and all energy levels.

What We do :)

Activities that Nourish Body, Mind & Soul.

  • 🧶 Knit & Create – crafting circles where creativity flows
  • 🎨 Paint & Express – guided and free-style art sessions
  • 📚 Read & Share – book swaps and cozy discussions
  • 🏃 Run & Move – from morning jogs to running groups
  • 🤸 Pilates & Flow – gentle strength and grounding
  • 💕 Friendship Speed-Dating – fun, structured ways to meet amazing new sisters
  • 🌈 Rainbow Picnic – a joyful outdoor gathering celebrating colour, food & connection
  • 🍷 Wine Tasting Nights – unwind, sip, and discover local flavours with your sisters

Why Join?

Because Life is Better in a Circle.

Our club isn't just about hobbies — it's about connection.

Here you'll find:

  • A safe, inclusive space for women of all ages
  • A variety of activities to fit your lifestyle
  • Opportunities to make lifelong friendships
  • A way to recharge and grow, together
